burp

everything happens by the water: curly hair, sock marks, snow that you confuse for the moon. pink soles of bare feet & oceanside grins. a tongue that is really a wave licking at the humidity in humanity. your hand reaching out to the version of yourself that stands in the ocean, dark water pulling at the bottoms of your jean pants, fingernails stuffed with beads of sand. eyes, tell me that you are happy. nose, scrunch up in joy. burp, laugh, sip. inhale. let the water tell you that it is yours.


(Originally written for Poetry I, spring 2021 under Imani Jackson)
