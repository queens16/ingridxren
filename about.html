about

Ingrid Ren grew up in the San Francisco Bay Area, attending French immersion schools. She took a gap year after high school and joined AmeriCorps NCCC to do service work with various nonprofits in California and Montana. At Brown University, she is currently studying English Creative Nonfiction Writing and hopes to impact people through her writing.
