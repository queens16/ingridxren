about

Ingrid Ren enjoys being in water, making people laugh, predicting movie plots, meticulously peeling grapefruits the way her dad did for her, and writing about the people in her life.
She dislikes shouting in order to be heard.
One day, Ingrid hopes to live in Philly, to publish a book, and to have memberships at many art museums. For now, she is, for the love of god, trying to establish a consistent writing practice while working as a paralegal for a nonprofit law firm in Boston.
